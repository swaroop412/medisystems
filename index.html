ABOUT US

Your Trusted Partner in Healthcare

Nellore Medi Systems Private Limited specializes in healthcare manufacturing, pharmaceutical distribution, medical equipment, hospital infrastructure, procurement, and healthcare consultancy.

Our mission is to provide integrated healthcare solutions that enable hospitals, clinics, medical colleges, pharmaceutical companies, government organizations, and healthcare institutions to operate more efficiently while delivering better patient care.

OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

We provide end-to-end healthcare solutions ranging from manufacturing and pharmaceutical distribution to hospital infrastructure, consultancy, procurement, and project management.

Healthcare Manufacturing

Premium quality sanitary products, contract manufacturing, private label manufacturing, institutional supply, and government tender support.

Pharmaceutical Distribution

Wholesale distribution of branded medicines, generic medicines, OTC products, vaccines, injectables, nutraceuticals, and hospital consumables.

Medical Equipment

Turnkey Hospital Projects

Procurement Solutions

Medical equipment, laboratory equipment, hospital furniture, surgical consumables, pharmaceutical products, and project procurement.
